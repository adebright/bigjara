About Bigjara

Big begins small

Bigjara is a research , computing , and vocational education initiative with primary interest in addressing Africa's skill shortage through the use of technology - by providing an accessible platform for learners and tutors , and by lowering the barrier involved in climbing up the ladder. We also build application, drive growth through analytics, and enable business scalability through cloud computing for small business owners, the public and enterprise use.

This initiative is led by ACE AFRICA with consitent and robust support from Adney Reich Nigeria Limited

ACE AFRICA provides the needed technical expertise required to fulfill the vision and mission of the initiative while Adney Reich helps in funding the initiative

The funding provided by Adney Reich helps in opening doors to many interested individuals across the continent since the cost of training becomes affordable

Understanding our program

Our training is subsidized by the funds we receive from Adney Reich Nigeria Limited. We provide housing , Internet service , mental , and emotional support to our students during the course of the training.

We make the training accessible to everyone by using a price mechanism that makes the program worthy , and also affordable.

We don't and will never offer free trainings. You pay for what you seek to gain.

Interested individuals or organizations with interest in subscribing to any of our courses , have access to 1 week of free trial.

We examine you base on your study performance after the one week of trial , and decide if you can continue the program , or you will have to go home

Those who are verified to continue after the one week of trial , are required to make their financial commitment for the program

We believe in what we offer and as such stick to a refund policy. We refund whatever we get from any individual if we happen to breach any of our pre-signed agreement. So , take time to carefully read through all of our policies , before registering to any of our courses. We do not accept those who don't read , or those , who have no time to verify information
